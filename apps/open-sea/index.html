Welcome to the Open Sea

```

The open sea is the largest ecosystem on Earth. It is vast and variable and includes ocean waters away from coastal limits and above the seafloor. The word pelagic refers to the open seas or oceans.

Coastal and seabed habitats are associated with tangible geological surfaces. The open sea is different because it is primarily defined by intangible conditions that cannot be touched but strongly shape the organisms living there.
