How to Use
1. Open the app from the MBA Hub or directly from apps/five-senses-pwa/index.html.
2. Use Search to find topics such as touch, kelp, sound, baleen, taste, or accessibility.
3. Open dropdowns for interpretation prompts.
4. Use Clear Cache after updating files.
5. Use Troubleshooting if the app shows old content or does not work correctly.

Known Issues
- This rebuild has not yet been user-tested on PC Chrome, iPhone Safari, or offline mode.
- Registry entry must be added to the Hub registry before the app appears in the Hub.
- Follow Monterey Bay Aquarium-approved guidance for any live animal, tactile, taste, smell, or guest interaction.
