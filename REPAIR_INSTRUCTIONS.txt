MBA Hub Repair Instructions

Use this package when the Whale Parasites app is appearing as the Hub.

1. Back up your current broken folder first.
2. Replace the entire MBA-hub folder with this MBA-hub folder, OR copy these root files into your existing MBA-hub root:
   - index.html
   - styles.css
   - app.js
   - data.js
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icon.svg
3. Make sure the apps folder stays at:
   MBA-hub/apps/
4. Make sure Whale Parasites is at:
   MBA-hub/apps/whale-parasites-associates/index.html
5. Do not put Whale Parasites index.html into the MBA-hub root.
6. After replacing files, open the site root. It should show MBA Hub, not Whale Parasites & Associates.
7. In Chrome, press Ctrl + Shift + R. On iPhone, clear Safari website data if the old page still appears.
