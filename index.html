Whale Parasites & Associates

Barnacles, whale lice, remoras, cookiecutter scars, and field ID clues.

Field note · June 2025

Super friendly humpbacks gave us great looks at barnacles, lice, and remora scars. Blue and fin whales running offshore brought cookiecutter shark bites and clusters of whalesuckers. Coastal species often show heavier parasite loads because drifting larvae are nearby.

Fast field cues

Round scarsCookiecutter shark
White clustersWhale barnacles
Tiny crustaceansWhale lice
Dark clinging fishWhalesucker remora
