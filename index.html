Troubleshooting

Refresh or Clear Cache

If an app shows old content, first try the Clear Cache button above.

On PC Chrome, press Ctrl + Shift + R for a hard refresh.

Return to Hub Gives 404

If Return to Hub gives a 404 error, check that the app button points to:

../../index.html

For apps inside apps/app-name/, this is usually the correct path.

App Still Shows Old Version

Use Clear Cache, then reload the app. If still old, close and reopen the browser.

ChatGPT Recents Missing in Chrome

Problem

Your ChatGPT Recents or conversation history are missing in Google Chrome, but they still appear on another device, such as your iPhone.

This usually means your conversations are still saved on your ChatGPT account. The issue is usually Chrome cache, cookies, site data, extensions, or a temporary sync problem.

Most Likely Solutions

  1. Hard refresh the page:
    Press Ctrl + Shift + R
  2. Sign out and back in:
    Sign out of ChatGPT, then sign back into the same account.
  3. Clear only ChatGPT site data:
    1. Open chrome://settings/content/all
    2. Search for chatgpt.com
    3. Delete the ChatGPT site data.
    4. Reload ChatGPT.
    5. Sign in again.
  4. Restart Chrome:
    Completely close Google Chrome and reopen it.
  5. Restart your PC:
    If the problem continues, restart your computer.

What Are You Seeing?

  • The left sidebar is completely blank.
  • It says Unable to load history.
  • There is a spinning/loading indicator.
  • The sidebar itself is missing.

Tip: A screenshot usually helps diagnose the problem faster.
